HIKING YONG BELAR

Mandatory Items

  • TWO headlamps with spare battery
  • Emergency blanket
  • Personal First Aid Kit
  • Food & hydration
  • Windbreaker
  • Raincoat

    • Failure to produce these items during item check prior to the starting point will render the trip cancelled, with no refunds.

Rules

  • The decision of the Mountain Guide or the Guide-in-command is final and non-negotiable on all matters likely to impact the safety and well-being of the participants.
  • Participants must comply to the advice of the Mountain Guide at all times.
  • Agree to participate from the beginning until the end, and commit 100% to the journey.

Terms of Engagement

  • For experienced G7 hikers only (strictly not for beginner/moderate hiker).
  • Cut off time (cot) at Kem Kasut at 1045hrs.
  • Participants who are unable to make it to Kem Kasut before the Cut-off Time (COT) will have to wait and stay/rest at Kem Kasut until further instructions from the Mountain Guide (Itinerary based on normal pace of G7 hikers).
  • Overtime charges at RM50/hour(s)/group to start after the 15th hour of hiking.
  • The Mountain Guide’s instructions should be adhered to at all times for the safety of all participants.
  • 4WD pick up time starts at 5am, return at 7pm and must complete as per group.
  • Should a Group arrive at the Starting Point at different time, the earlier cohort of the Group will bear the additional costs for the transfer to Masjid Lojing should the cohort choose to not wait for the later cohort.
Day Trip - YB SPEED DATE

  • FOR THOSE WITH LESS TIME TO SPEND

    It is possible to summit Mount Yong Belar on a day trip, but we strongly recommend this for those with speed on the trails. It is estimated to take 12 to 15 hours for an average hiker to summit and to return to the starting point, therefore requiring to cover some parts of the trail in the dark.

    • 0400hrs Meet up at Masjid Lojing
    • 0415hrs 4WD to Starting Point
    • 0445hrs Arrive at Kebun, briefing, & warm up
    • 0500hrs Hike Starts
    • 0700hrs Kem Tudung Periuk
    • (re-group, solat & fast breakfast, last water point)
    • 0730hrs Trekking continues
    • 0930hrs Arrive at Kem Kasut
    • (Rest & re-group 15min) – Cut-off Time @ 1045hrs
    • 0945hrs Depart Kem Kasut – summit push
    • 1045hrs Summit Yong Belar (lunch & photo session)
    • 1215hrs Descend
    • 12.50hrs Arrive Kem Kasut
    • 1420hrs Arrive Kem Tudung periuk
    • 1600hrs Pintu Rimba & return 4WD
    • 1700hrs Drop off at Masjid Lojing
Cost Per Pax:
  • RM165 (inclusive of insurance and certificate of completion)
  • RM150 (without insurance and certificate of completion)

Prices quoted are not inclusive of food and beverages.

2 Days 1 Night - YB Introduction

  • FOR THOSE WITH A LITTLE MORE TIME

    To suit the ever busy lifestyle of the urban trail-seekers, compressed hiking trips are becoming a norm. With a two days hike, you will experience Mount Yong Belar by putting up a night in the montane forests, with the peaceful chorus of the insects as your lullaby, after the summit push.

  • Day 1

    • 0630hrs Meet up point Masjid Lojing
    • 0700hrs 4WD to Starting Point
    • 0730hrs Briefing & warm up
    • 0800hrs Start hike
    • 0810hrs Pintu Rimba
    • 1000hrs Kem Tudung Periuk (campsite)
    • 1100hrs Depart Kem Tudung Periuk – summit push
    • 1400hrs Kem Kasut
    • 1500hrs Summit Yong Belar (group photo)
    • 1530hrs Descend
    • 1630hrs Kem Kasut
    • 1900hrs Kem Tudung Periuk (overnight)

    Day 2

    • 0800hrs Kem Tudung Periuk (pack, clean up camspite)
    • 1000hrs Descend from Kem Tudung Periuk
    • 1300hrs Pintu Rimba & return 4WD
    • 1400hrs Drop off at Masjid Lojing
Cost Per Pax:

2 Days 1 Night - YB Immersion

  • FOR THOSE WHO WANTS MORE

    Why rush when there are so much more that Yong Belar could give? This trail takes you off course to the Pelaur Waterfall where you will set up a base, so that you can immerse yourself in the true beauty of Yong Belar. Dip yourself in the cold spring waters of Pelaur Waterfall after the workout, and wake up fresh to the sound of the flowing waters.

    Strongly recommended for those who seek joy from spending time with nature, and the people around you, without the distraction of human progress named technology.

3 Days 2 Nights - YB Ultimate

  • FOR THOSE WHERE MORE IS NEVER ENOUGH

    Mount Yong Belar is only one of the peaks along the Titiwangsa Mountains range, and there are trails that connects to the surrounding summits. You will be summiting Mount Warpu on your first day, en route to the campsite, before putting up a night at Kem Tudung Periuk.

    Your Day 2 daypack will begin early, stopping by at the Pelaur waterfall before continuing on the summit push. You will then return to the same campsite to rest for the day, before packing up and descend the next morning.

  • Day 1

    • 0600hrs Meet up point at Masjid Lojing
    • 0800hrs 4WD to Starting Point
    • 0900hrs Briefing & warm up
    • 1000hrs Simpang warpu
    • 1030hrs Mount warpu
    • 1100hrs Simpang warpu
    • 1300hrs Puncak Tipu 3
    • 1500hrs Kem Tudung Periuk (campsite)

    Day 2

    • 0600hrs Prepare for daypack
    • 0730hrs Start hike
    • 0830hrs Pelaur Waterfall
    • 1100hrs Kem Kasut
    • 1200hrs Summit Yong Belar (group photo)
    • 1300hrs Descend
    • 1400hrs Kem Kasut
    • 1800hrs Kem Tudung Periuk (campsite)

    Day 3

    • 0730hrs Kem Tudung Periuk (pack, clean up camspite)

Ultimate Daypack - Yong Belar 3 Boundaries

  • FOR THE REAL TOUGH ONES

    The Yong Belar 3 Boundaries (YB3B) trail is an extended trail of Yong Belar, where it forms part of the Trans Titiwangsa version 2 trail. Moving along the spine of Peninsular Malaysia, expect unending ascends and descends, traversing across the Titiwangsa Range. This is the same trail of the YB3B Challenge.

    • 1540hrs Summit Mount 6040
    • 1740hrs Pintu Rimba & return 4WD
